Energy: Analyzing consumption in the period

Consumption statistics and time trends can be viewed for a given period in the Graph page.

The available values are at the plant, group or single meter levels:

  • Current year consumption or previous year consumption, with monthly details.

  • Monthly consumption in the current year, with daily details.

  • Weekly consumption in the current year with daily details.

Analyzing consumption: fixed cost settings

Three graphs are displayed on the page if Fixed cost settings are used:

  • the percent consumption, power, energy, costs and corresponding CO2

  • details of consumption average in kW

  • details of consumption in kWh

Analyzing consumption: setting up time slots

Six graphs are displayed on the page if Time Slot settings are used:

  • the percent consumption, power, energy, costs and corresponding CO2

  • details of consumption average in kW

  • details of consumption in kWh

  • percent consumption and costs for each cost category

  • total consumption and costs for each cost category

  • total consumption for each cost category with daily details (weekly and monthly report) or monthly details (annual report)
